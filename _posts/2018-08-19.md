You may not know this about me, but I'm kind of a weeb. Its pretty shameful actually, especially since I look down on weebs, but my stuggles my weebery is not the the point of this post. As my time as a closet weeb I read a great number of manga from a variety of genre, some were good and others not so much. Like everyone I slowly built up a list of manga that I liked; my list contains many of the same manga that that others have on their lists: Berserk, Full Metal Alchemist, 20th Century Boys, and Grand Blue. Filled with a mixture of comedy and drama my list . But the two manga that I would consider my top two are defintely not on everyone else lists are D-Frag and All-Rounder Meguru. 

For this post I'm only going to talk about All-Rounder Megure (ARM), as talking about both D-Frag and it would be rather difficult as one is a comedy and the other is a sports drama.
